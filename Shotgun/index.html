Benelli M2

  • Barrels :18.5",[2] 21", 24", 26", 28"

Benelli Nova



Benelli Nova

The Benelli Nova is a pump action shotgun, used for hunting and self-defense. It has a one-piece receiver and buttstock, made of steel-reinforced polymer. This model is intended for defensive purposes. With an 18½" barrel, and rifle or ghost- ring (diopter) sights, it is easier to wield and quicker to sight than hunting models.

Specifications

  • Type: Pump action shotgun
  • Place of origin : Italy
  • Manufacturer: Benelli
  • Variants: Supernova
  • Mass: 8 lbs. (3.63kg)
  • Length: 45.5-49.5 in. (1,155.7-1,257.3mm)
  • Barrel length: 18½-28 in. (609.6-711.2mm)
  • Cartridge: 12-gauge shotshell, 20-Gauge Shotshell
  • Action: Pump-action
  • Feed system: 4+1 internal magazine

BERETTA DT-10



BERETTA DT-10

The Beretta DT10 is an over and under shotgun. It is manufactured, marketed, and distributed by Fabbrica d'Armi Pietro Beretta, in Gardone Val Trompia, Italy. It is one of the few Beretta shotguns that has a trigger unit that is detachable in the field, the DT in its name standing for detachable trigger. It combines features between the older Beretta ASE and the newer Beretta 682 model and is targeted at their competition market.

Specifications

  • Type: Double barreled shotgun
  • Place of origin : Italy
  • Manufacturer: Beretta
  • Mass: 8 lbs.
  • Barrel length: 28, 30, 32 or 34 inches
  • Caliber : 12 gauge
